SCAM 2.0
Entrepreneurship Cell

This event is designed to complement the innovative ideas, analytical skills and problem solving ability of the candidates.The event also aims to help candidates identify various obstacles and pressures that creates hurdles in the path of building a company , and to reflect on ways to avoid those.

It would basically be a ground event which will be a kind of corporate commotion in which students will be divided in teams and each team would have some dealers and some entrepreneurs who will aim to establish their companies with the help of provided commodities and services by negotiating, selling and investing amongst each other. The team with the largest capital value at the end of the game would be declared as the winners.

Mr. And Miss Aayam 2018
Club Ojaswa

Mr. And Miss. Aayam 2k18 comprises of 3 rounds. First round is treasure hunt in which the teams will find clues hidden in the capmus premises to find the treasure. Second round is Show your Popularity in which the teams will be segregated and each individual will post his/her picture on the facebook page of our event and collect maximum likes possible.
Third round is the stage round where the qualified participants will come on the stage and an extempore will be conducted. After which the judges will ask them some questions to judge their personality, on the basis of which Mr. And Miss Aayam will be announced.
